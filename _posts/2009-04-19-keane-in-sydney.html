Yesterday we went to Keane's concert in Sydney. It's one of my favorite bands and it was one of the best performances I've ever seen.

Tom Chaplin and the pianist Tim Rice Oxley have an amazing contagious energy on stage

They played songs from the current album and from the old ones.
One of the most exciting moments was when Tim went to the crowd singing "Wrap yourself around me..." from the new album Perfect Symmetry.

Check it out...




We went with Anne and Mark Needham.
